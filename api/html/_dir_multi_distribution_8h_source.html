9 /*
10  \class DirMultiDistribution
11  \author Castronovo Michael
12 
13  \brief A MDP distribution represented by a Dirichlet Multinomial
14  distribution.
15  It has been designed to address:
16  - DISCRETE state space (int)
17  - DISCRETE action space (int)
18  - SINGLE reward (double)
19 
20  The state space, the action space, the reward function and
21  the initial state are identical for all the MDPs composing the
22  support of this distribution.
23  They only differ by their transition matrix, which is drawn
24  from a Dirichlet Multinomial distribution. It defines
25  a Dirichlet distribution for each state-action pair.
26  The DM distribution can be updated. It consists to increment
27  the observation counters.
28 
29  \date 2014-12-13
30 */
